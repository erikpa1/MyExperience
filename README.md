## 1. Utilization of Indoor RTLS and IoT in Manufacturing and Logistics Companies
**Abstract:** Article presents the concepts of RTLS – Real Time Location System for manufacturing and logistics companies, focusing on their need in contemporary enterprises. RTLS enables real-time tracking of personnel and assets in a company for optimization and management. The article also presents various new functionalities and possibilities associated with this system.

**Authors**: Erik Palenčík, Štefan Mozol, Patrik Grznár

**DOI**: 10.53052/9788367652506.07
